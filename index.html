About Us

FlyUC is a student organization at the College of Engineering and Applied Science, University of Cincinnati. The FlyUC team was initally formed for the GoFly Prize in 2017.  FlyUC is a proud GoFly Phase III Team.

Today, FlyUC continues to work on designing and developing vertical take-off and landing aircrafts. The object of the organization is to create a platform for students interested in vertical flight.

The team is comprised of four subteams: Structures, Electronics, Propulsion, and Operations. We are open to all University of Cincinnati students, regardless of majors and colleges.
